HOW PHILADELPHIA CAN SOAR PAST THE CHIEFS

Sunday, 9th February 2025

GO Eagles GO!

Justin Ralph Gocatek

The stage is set for an electrifying showdown between the Philadelphia Eagles and the Kansas City Chiefs. With two of the league's top teams colliding, Philadelphia will need a meticulous game plan to take down Patrick Mahomes and company. Here's how the Eagles can secure a statement victory:

Win the Turnover Battle

Championship-caliber games often come down to key turnovers. The Eagles' defense must capitalize on any opportunities to strip the ball or pick off Mahomes. Defensive backs like Darius Slay and James Bradberry must be aggressive in coverage, jumping routes and forcing errant throws. The defensive line must also focus on generating strip sacks and fumbles, creating additional possession opportunities..

Likewise, Hurts must protect the football, making smart decisions under pressure and avoiding costly giveaways that could swing momentum in the Chiefs favor. Winning the turnover margin will be critical in dictating the game's outcome.

Contain Travis Kelce

Stopping Mahomes is a tall task, but limiting his go-to target, Travis Kelce, is imperative. A combination of linebackers and safeties must blanket the All-Pro tight end, disrupting his routes and preventing crucial third-down conversions. If the Eagles can make the Chiefs rely on secondary weapons, their defense will have the upper hand. while safeties such as Reed Blankenship should provide over-the-top coverage to prevent deep completions. Additionally, defensive coordinator Sean Desai must implement a mix of zone and man coverage to keep Kelce guessing, forcing Mahomes to look elsewhere. If the Eagles can make the Chiefs rely on secondary weapons, their defense will have the upper hand.

Control the Clock with the Ground Game

A dominant rushing attack is the Eagles' ticket to victory. By leaning on D'Andre Swift and Kenneth Gainwell, Philadelphia can dictate the pace of the game, keeping Mahomes and the Chiefs' offense off the field. Sustained drives and ball control will limit Kansas City's explosive play opportunities.

Mixing in designed quarterback runs for Jalen Hurts will also keep the defense on its heels. Additionally, incorporating short-yardage power runs and screen passes will help sustain drives and wear down the CHIEFS defensive front over time. Effective time management and efficient execution on third down will be crucial in keeping Mahomes on the sidelines and maximizing scoring opportunities.
